About me

I'm a self-taught artist who has always had a passion for creating. Growing up, I spent countless hours drawing, painting, and experimenting with different mediums, always striving to improve my skills and creativity. Over time, I developed a fascination with the texture and warmth of wood, and I began to incorporate it into my artwork. I experimented with various techniques, from carving and burning the wood to incorporating it into mixed media pieces. This led me to combine my love for wood with digital art, creating a whole new style that is truly my own. My artwork showcases my unique creativity and ability to blend different mediums in innovative ways. Each piece tells a unique story, with the wood adding a layer of texture and depth to my digital art. As a tattoo artist, I specialize in neo-traditional tattoos that draw inspiration from traditional designs but with a modern twist. I'm passionate about creating custom designs that are tailored to my clients' individual tastes and personalities, with each tattoo being a unique work of art.
